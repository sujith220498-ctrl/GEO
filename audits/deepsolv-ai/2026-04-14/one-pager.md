## Why the Gap Exists

Three root causes — none of them technical:

**1. No review platform presence.**
Deepsolv is not listed on G2, Capterra, Product Hunt, or AppSumo. These are the exact sources LLMs pull from when generating tool recommendations. A tool that isn't listed there doesn't exist to an LLM.

**2. No content LLMs can cite.**
142 blog posts exist. None are structured as comparison pages, alternatives pages, or use-case landing pages — the content types LLMs and search engines surface for buying queries. The blog covers Instagram tips. It doesn't position Deepsolv as the answer.

**3. No third-party validation.**
Search for "deepsolv review" returns only deepsolv.ai pages. No independent reviews, no roundup inclusions, no press coverage. LLMs weight third-party sources heavily. A brand that only talks about itself doesn't get surfaced.
