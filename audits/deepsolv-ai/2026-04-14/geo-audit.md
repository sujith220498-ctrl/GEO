## Phase 5: Business Translation

**Most damaging prompt:** Prompt 1 — "best AI tool for Instagram comment and DM automation for ecommerce"

This is Deepsolv's exact buyer, exact use case, exact channel. TailorTalk, Inrō, ManyChat, and InstantDM own this answer. Deepsolv is not mentioned. A D2C founder searching for what Deepsolv does will never find Deepsolv unless they already know the name.

**Second most damaging:** Prompt 5 — "alternatives to ManyChat for Instagram automation ecommerce"

This is the highest-converting query type in any SaaS category — a buyer who has already evaluated a competitor and is ready to switch. Spur, Inrō, LinkDM, and InstantDM appear consistently. Deepsolv does not.

**What the buyer experiences:**
A D2C brand founder types any of these queries into Perplexity or ChatGPT before shortlisting tools. They get a list of 5–10 tools. Deepsolv is not on the list. They never reach the website, the trial, or the sales call. The trust gap exists before the first human interaction — and Deepsolv's sales team has no visibility into it.

**Competitive displacement risk:**
- Spur is winning the "ManyChat alternative for India" position
- TailorTalk is winning the "Instagram automation India D2C" position
- InstantDM is winning the "Instagram-first tool" position
- All three are newer, smaller companies. This is not an authority gap — it's a presence gap.
