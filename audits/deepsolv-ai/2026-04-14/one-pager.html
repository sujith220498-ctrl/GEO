Root Cause

Why Deepsolv is absent.

LLMs do not cite company websites. Over 90% of AI citations come from third-party sources — review platforms, roundup articles, comparison pages. Deepsolv is not present on any of them.

What Changes the Data

Concrete steps, by effort.

Two phases. The first requires no content creation. The second builds the pages that compound in both search and AI visibility over time.

This week — no content required
  • List on G2 — free, takes a week to go live. Single highest-impact action. Every competitor is there. Deepsolv is not.
  • List on Capterra — free listing. Cited for "software alternatives" queries across every category.
  • Launch on Product Hunt — free, establishes a third-party presence LLMs can cite.
  • Submit to chatimize.com and instantdm.com roundups — both appeared in multiple prompts tested. Reach out for inclusion.
Next 4 weeks — three pages that compound
  • "Deepsolv vs ManyChat" comparison page — this is the single highest-converting query type in SaaS. A buyer who is already evaluating ManyChat and searching for alternatives is ready to switch. Own the answer.
  • "Best ManyChat alternative for India" landing page — Spur currently owns this angle. Deepsolv has a stronger product for D2C Instagram automation. The page doesn't exist. Build it.
  • Fix the existing automation tools blog post — /blog/top-7-instagram-automation-tools ranks at position 42 for "instagram automation tool" (1,600 searches/month). Deepsolv is not positioned as the primary recommendation in its own roundup article. This is fixable in a day.
Ongoing — monthly cadence
  • Run 10-prompt GEO audit monthly across Perplexity, ChatGPT, Gemini.
  • Track: visibility %, which tools dominate each prompt, which third-party URLs are cited.
  • One new comparison or alternatives page per month.
  • One new review platform submission per month.
