When a DISCOM CTO consults an AI assistant before shortlisting vendors, WorkOnGrid is not in the conversation. A preference is already partially formed by the time they contact anyone — and it is not WorkOnGrid.

4 / Root Cause

Why the Gap Exists

LLMs do not primarily cite company websites. Over 97% of AI citations come from third-party sources — review platforms, industry publications, comparison roundups.

WorkOnGrid is listed on Capterra. It is not listed on:

1
Gartner Peer Insights

Where "alternatives to Fluentgrid" queries pull their vendor lists. Oracle, BCITS, and Trilliant appear here. WorkOnGrid does not.

2
aimultiple.com and startus-insights.com

Cited for "best smart meter solutions" and "emerging utility tech" queries. These two pages alone supplied competitors across three of the five prompts tested.

3
tndindia.com, mercomindia.com, electricalindia.in

The India-specific publications Perplexity cited. Bidgely earned the #1 DISCOM India recommendation because a single article on tndindia.com covered their product launch. That one third-party page was enough.

5 / Action Plan

What Needs to Happen

Immediate — no content creation required
  • Submit to Gartner Peer Insights — free listing, 2–3 weeks to go live. Directly addresses the highest-impact prompt gap.
  • Get listed on aimultiple.com and startus-insights.com — smart meter and utility tech roundups. These two pages appear in LLM responses for three of the five buyer queries tested.
  • Pitch product coverage to tndindia.com, mercomindia.com, electricalindia.in — the exact sources Perplexity already cites for DISCOM India queries.
Short Term — content creation required
  • Build a "WorkOnGrid vs Fluentgrid" comparison page — directly captures the highest-intent competitive displacement query.
  • Consolidate blog content around each use case — one authoritative guide per use case, structured so LLMs can confidently cite it.
  • Update the energy data management blog post — 60,500 monthly searches, currently rank 79. One optimisation pass has significant traffic upside.
Ongoing
  • Run a 10-prompt LLM audit monthly across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini.
  • Refresh blog content every 4–5 months — content older than this decays in both Google rankings and LLM citations.
  • Track which third-party pages competitors appear in and ensure WorkOnGrid is on those same pages.
