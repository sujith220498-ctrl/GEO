3 / Root Cause

Why WorkOnGrid is absent

LLMs do not cite company websites. Over 97% of AI citations come from third-party sources — review platforms, industry publications, comparison pages. WorkOnGrid is listed on Capterra. It does not appear on the sources that these queries pull from.
