## 3. Why This Is the Case

LLMs do not cite company websites. The data shows that over 97% of AI citations come from third-party sources — review platforms, industry publications, comparison pages.

WorkOnGrid is listed on Capterra. It does not appear on:

| Source | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Gartner Peer Insights | The source Perplexity pulls "alternatives to Fluentgrid" from |
| aimultiple.com | Cited for smart meter and utility tech roundups |
| startus-insights.com | Cited for emerging utility tech queries |
| tndindia.com | The source that made Bidgely the top DISCOM India recommendation |

Bidgely earned the first-position DISCOM India recommendation from one article on tndindia.com. That article covered their product launch. No other content was required.
